About Us

Welcome to Hazelwood Estate Agents, your distinguished partner in the world of real estate, committed to providing unparalleled services across the United Kingdom and India. In the UK, our office is conveniently located at Suite 1, Marlborough House, 32-36 Hazelwood Rd, Northampton NN1 1LN. In India, find us at the Tas Building, Aluva-Perumbavoor Rd, Perumbavoor, Kerala 683542.

At Hazelwood, we redefine real estate excellence, focusing on physical presence and personalized services. Our aim is to transform your property aspirations into a seamless and fulfilling reality.

In the United Kingdom, we specialize in connecting buyers with their dream homes and sellers with lucrative opportunities. Our expert team, located in the heart of Northampton, possesses in-depth market knowledge, ensuring optimal results for every transaction. Whether you are seeking a cozy residence or a strategic investment, Hazelwood in the UK stands as your trusted guide.

In India, our presence in Perumbavoor, Kerala, reflects our commitment to catering to the diverse real estate needs of the region. From traditional Kerala houses to modern apartments, we offer a curated selection that embraces the unique charm of Kerala's real estate. Hazelwood in India is your local expert, dedicated to connecting tenants with their ideal living or business spaces.

Our suite of services includes comprehensive sales and mortgage assistance, providing guidance from the initial investment to the final property sale. Hazelwood prioritizes client satisfaction, delivering tailored solutions with integrity and professionalism.

Moreover, our extended opening hours until 9 pm UK time demonstrate our dedication to accessibility and personalized service. We go beyond conventional real estate practices, investing in the UK, India, and Dubai, and establishing connections with top-line builders in these regions.
